Hi! I am Arka Ray. I am currently doing my PhD from Computer Science and Automation department of IISc, Bengaluru (visit CSA @ IISc), under Anand Louis. I also did my masters from IISc and did my B. Tech from University of Calcutta. I am broadly interested in Theoretical Computer Science. I currently work on Approximation Algorithms. I am specially interested in learning more about Sum of Squares Hierarchy, and Simplicial Complexes. Arindam Khan and I worked on showing inapproximability results for various packing and covering problems as part of my MTech Project.
